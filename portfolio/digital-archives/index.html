Digital Archives Development

  • Skills Needed:
    HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, Omeka, Collective Access, MODS
  • Client:
    Various
  • Years:
    2013-present

I work as a consultant developing digital archives websites and related instructional programming. Samples here include the Weeksville Heritage Center’s digital archives, a digital timeline exhibit celebrating the 50th anniversary of Queens College civil rights activism, an Omeka merchant mariners’ oral history website browsable by map, tag cloud, and person, and SUNY Maritime Library’s Collective Access website using the MODS metadata profile.
